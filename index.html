The SINK ELON Story

To most people it may have just looked and seemed like any other ordinary bathroom sink, but inside sources at Twitter have reported to us that this was no ordinary sink! It was in fact a new AI-powered sink that Musk has been working on for many years now. Codenamed "Sinky", reports suggest that the device will be become the AI friend you can really open up to with your thoughts and feelings when you're brushing your teeth. Let that sink in! It was claimed that Sinky being hand-delivered into Twitter Offices, represented "One small step for sinks, one giant leap for bathroom fixtures", with the ultimate goal being for the device to eventually drive the SpaceX shuttle to Mars. Resports suggest that insiders have already had numerous conversations with the porcelain fixture, with some interesting responses coming back. For example, when Sinky was asked what his favourite sport was, he responded "Sinkronized swimming". Wehn we was asked what his favourite time of the year is, he had no hestitation on stating "Sinko de Mayo". His favourite music band is "Sink 182" and his favourite song is Ed Sheeran's "Sinking Out Loud".
